==behavioral==
Teamwork & Collaboration||Working with cross-functional teams (PM, design, data); Handling disagreement with a teammate or lead; Contributing in code/design reviews||E:Tell me about a time you worked well in a team.||M:Describe a time you disagreed with a technical decision — how did you handle it?||T:Tell me about a time a team conflict significantly impacted a project deadline, and how you resolved it.
Ownership & Accountability||Owning mistakes/production incidents; Taking initiative beyond assigned scope; Following through on commitments||E:Tell me about a project you owned end-to-end.||M:Describe a mistake you made at work and how you handled it.||T:Tell me about a time you caused a significant production issue — walk through the incident, the root cause, and what changed afterward.
Problem Solving Under Ambiguity||Handling unclear requirements; Prioritization under time pressure; Dealing with incomplete data/information||E:Tell me about a time you had to solve a problem with limited information.||M:Describe a time priorities changed midway through a project — how did you adapt?||T:Tell me about the most ambiguous problem you've faced professionally and how you structured your approach to it.
Learning & Adaptability||Picking up new tools/technologies quickly; Responding to critical feedback; Staying current in a fast-moving field (AI/ML)||E:How do you stay updated with new developments in AI/ML?||M:Tell me about a time you had to learn a new tool or framework quickly to deliver a project.||T:Describe a time feedback fundamentally changed your approach to a project, and how you handled receiving it.
Communication & Stakeholder Management||Explaining technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders; Managing expectations on timelines/scope; Delivering bad news (delays, failures)||E:How do you explain a technical concept to a non-technical stakeholder?||M:Tell me about a time you had to manage a stakeholder's expectations on a delayed deliverable.||T:Describe a time you had to deliver bad news (a missed deadline, a failed model, a bad result) to leadership — how did you frame it?
Conflict Resolution & Leadership||Mediating disagreements between team members; Mentoring junior team members; Leading without formal authority||E:Tell me about a time you helped a teammate who was struggling.||M:Describe a situation where you had to lead a project without formal authority over the team.||T:Tell me about the hardest interpersonal conflict you've had to resolve on a team, and what you'd do differently now.
